## About Me

I'm a **Senior Software Engineer** with 14+ years building scalable distributed systems across **wealth management, e-commerce, healthcare, and compliance** domains.

Right now I'm building a production AI application at **Intuit TurboTax** on the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** — shipping tax tools directly into ChatGPT and Claude that served **10K+ users** and drove **2K+ paid tax filings** in the April 2026 tax peak.

I care deeply about clean architecture, measurable impact, and shipping things that actually work at scale.
